Preliminary cost ranges are derived from recent CMA project bids in the Birmingham, Alabama market. Tier numbers reflect $/sf of heated & cooled area; the 15% uplift covers wall thicknesses and miscellaneous circulation per CMA's standard area calculations. Material adjustments are computed on under-roof SF. The steep-lot adjustment is a 5% overall surcharge intended to cover deeper foundations, retaining walls, and added site work. Figures are construction cost only — no land, design fees, pools, landscaping, furnishings, or sales tax. Carlisle Moore Architects, Inc.
